# HyperArc

A blockchain-based cross-border stock trading platform that leverages RWA (Real World Assets) technology to tokenize traditional financial assets.

## Overview

HyperArc is an innovative cross-border stock trading platform designed to address the challenges of traditional international stock investment, including low settlement efficiency, high costs, and limited liquidity. By integrating blockchain technology with traditional financial markets, we provide investors with faster, more transparent, and cost-effective cross-border investment solutions.
